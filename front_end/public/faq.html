Frequently Asked Questions

1. How accurate is the weather data?

Our forecasts are powered by trusted global weather APIs and updated in real-time. While accuracy can vary due to sudden weather changes, we aim to provide the most reliable information possible.

2. Do I need to enable location services?

Location services help us provide weather updates specific to your current area. However, you can still search for any city manually if you prefer not to enable location.

3. Is the app free to use?

Yes! Weatherly is free to use. Some optional premium features may be introduced in the future to enhance your experience.

4. Can I check weather in multiple cities?

Absolutely. You can search for weather in any city worldwide. Your recent searches are also saved for quick access.

5. How often is the forecast updated?

Weather data is refreshed every 15 minutes to ensure you always see the latest information.

6. Who do I contact for support?

You can reach our support team at support@weatherly.com . We’re happy to help!
