Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to WEATHERLY. Your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our weather app.

2. Information We Collect

  • Location data (if you grant permission) to provide accurate forecasts.
  • Basic device and browser information for app performance.
  • Optional user inputs like city names when searching weather.

3. How We Use Your Information

The data we collect is used solely to improve your experience. This includes providing real-time weather forecasts, improving app performance, and personalizing the content displayed to you.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. Data may be shared only with trusted service providers to ensure the app functions properly.

5. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data. However, please note that no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

6. Your Choices

You can choose to disable location services at any time through your device settings. You may also contact us if you would like your data removed from our systems.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here with an updated effective date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out at support@weathernow.com.
