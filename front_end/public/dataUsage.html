Data Usage

1. Weather Data Sources

Our app collects weather information from trusted third-party APIs. This includes data such as temperature, humidity, wind speed, sunrise and sunset times, and forecast information. We do not alter or manipulate this data, ensuring you receive accurate and up-to-date weather conditions.

2. Location Data

If you enable location services, we use your deviceâ€™s location to provide weather updates tailored to your current area. This information is processed in real-time and never stored permanently on our servers. You may also search for any city manually if you choose not to share your location.

3. Personal Data

We respect your privacy. Our app does not require personal data such as name, email, or contact details for basic use. Any information you voluntarily provide (e.g., through contact forms) is used solely for support purposes.

4. Data Storage & Security

Weather and forecast data are cached temporarily to improve app performance and reduce bandwidth usage. We implement security practices to protect all transmitted data from unauthorized access.

5. Third-Party Services

Our app may integrate third-party APIs and analytics tools to improve services. These services may collect anonymous usage data (such as app interactions) but will never access your personal information without consent.

6. Questions About Data Usage

If you have questions or concerns about how your data is used, please reach out to us at privacy@weathernow.com .
